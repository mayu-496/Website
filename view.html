Lewa Downs

It's a short flight from Nairobi to Lewa Downs, yet there will be plenty to see. Passing the heights of Mount Kenya, you’ll land in a lush nature reserve where big game thrives. The short transfer from the airstrip to your intimate Lewa Safari Camp doubles as a game drive. Be sure to keep your eyes peeled to see what wildlife you can tick off first!
